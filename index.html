System Methodology FAQ

系統核心演算法與架構說明 (FAQ)

1. Live Diagnosis (AI Commentary)

When clicking a team, your browser runs a dynamic contextual packaging query. It securely binds the team's real-time Points ($PTS$), Matches Played ($P$), and Goal Difference ($GD$) directly into a secure payload transmitted to the Gemini 2.5 Flash Engine. The AI behaves like a veteran sports quants analytics expert, returning tactical evaluation streams.

2. Predictive Odds (1st / 2nd Place)

The percentages displayed in your main matrix grids utilize an active Dynamic Performance Matrix Tracker. Rather than showing rigid historic baselines, our native mathematical engine calculates an ongoing momentum score based on current goal margins.

3. Public Retail Exchange Bet Volume

This section utilizes a data fusion network. Every 60 seconds, the site parses international sports exchanges via The Odds API. It pulls active moneyline prices, strips away the house overround margin mathematically, and processes the remaining value into a clean $100\%$ global volume handle.

1. 數據模型預測診斷分析 (AI 評論)

當您點擊任何球隊時，系統會立即包裝該球隊的即時數據，包括積分 ($PTS$)、已賽場次 ($P$) 及淨勝球 ($GD$)，並將這些上下文數據安全傳送至 Gemini 2.5 Flash 引擎。AI 將化身為資深體育量化分析師，針對該國的晉級形勢生成獨一無二的戰術報告。

2. 晉級小組第 1 / 第 2 名概率

主矩陣表格中顯示的百分比均採用系統內建的動態表現矩陣追蹤演算法。這套原生數學模型會根據球隊目前的淨勝球、積分形勢以及剩餘比賽的潛在權重，即時更新其最終出線的概率模型。

3. 全球交易所即時大眾投注份額

此區塊採用數據融合網路技術。系統每 60 秒會透過 The Odds API 抓取全球主流零售博彩公司盤口並自動扣除莊家的抽水利潤（Overround Margin），轉化為 $100\%$ 的清晰投注占比圖以維護渲染。
